Cordination

Coordination is the process of organizing people or groups to work together properly and effectively. It involves the harmonious functioning of parts to achieve a common goal or result. By coordinating their efforts, team members can divide tasks, share responsibilities, and leverage each other's strengths to deliver high-quality results.

Honesty

Honesty is a vital trait in a tech team as it fosters trust and transparency among team members. When team members are honest with each other, they can openly discuss and resolve issues, leading to better collaboration and problem-solving.

Learning

The learning process in a tech team involves continuous knowledge sharing, experimentation, and feedback. It requires an open-minded and curious attitude, where team members are willing to learn from each other's strengths and weaknesses.
